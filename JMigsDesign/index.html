About Jeremy

Jeremy Miragliotta has worked as an In-House Artist for Vanderbilts Sports and Spirits from 2007 to 2011, designing digital marketing animations as well as print design promoting the establishments specials and events. Jeremy continues to freelance design for a variety of clients meeting their needs for logo creation, poster advertisement, business card etc. Jeremy is also experienced in web design using HTML coding, CCS styling design, and Bootstrap framework as well. Jeremy continues education on the latest desgin concepts, platforms and programs to broaden his skillset as a designer and future developer.
