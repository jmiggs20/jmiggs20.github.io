About Jeremy

Jeremy Miragliotta graduated from Fairleigh Dickinson University in 2008 with a Bachelors Degree in Computer Graphics and a Minor is Advertising. He worked as an In-House Artist for Vanderbilts Sports and Spirits from 2007 to 2011, designing digital marketing animations as well as print design promoting the establishments specials and events. Currently, Jeremy works in education and is highly experienced with designing educational resources such a clearly formatted notes, graphic organizers, instructional guides, novel studies, posters, and building bulletin board presentations and decor. Throughout this time Jeremy has continued to freelance design for a variety of clients meeting their needs for logo creation, poster advertisement, business card etc. Jeremy is also experienced in web design using HTML coding, CCS styling design and Flash animations. His photographic editing skills include color correction, retouching and manipulations.
